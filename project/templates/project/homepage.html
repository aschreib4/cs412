Welcome to My Kitchen!

The Purpose of this Application

The goal of this application is to make cooking for college students just a little bit easier!

On this site, you'll be able to do a couple things:

  • Login and logout
  • Keep track of your kitchen inventory
  • Upload recipes and view recipes made by other users
  • Get shown which recipes you can make based on what you own--or, see what you're missing!
  • Group together recipes however you please--favorites, dinner meals, etc.!
